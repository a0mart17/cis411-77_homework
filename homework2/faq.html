Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the calculator?

The calculator can perform math functions quickly and easily. The most common functions are addition (+), subtraction (-), multiplication (x) and division (/). Press the “=” sign to get the answer. When you’re ready to do another math function, press “CE” to clear the screen.
